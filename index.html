Our community has members that are experienced in different fields and are friendly to approach.


Promote Open-Source

We promote contributing to and using Open-Source Software.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to provide a platform for the people who share our interest of Free and Open Source Software. Get new people involved by spreading awareness and generating interest. We also strive to provide a space where people can discuss ideas, ask for help and help others in the field of FOSS.
